ConcisePDF Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Data Processing

ConcisePDF is designed with privacy in mind. Here's how we handle your data:

PDF Processing

  • When you upload a PDF, the text is extracted locally in your browser
  • The extracted text is sent to OpenAI's API for summarization
  • We do not store your PDFs or their contents anywhere
  • All processing is temporary and data is discarded after summarization

Data Collection

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not track your usage of the extension
  • We do not store any of your documents or summaries

Third-Party Services

We use OpenAI's API for text summarization. Their privacy policy can be found at: https://openai.com/privacy
